## JOINS:-

\----------------

TO RETRIEVE THE DATA FROM MULTIPLE TABLES SIMULTANEOUSLY.



##### TYPES OF JOINS:

\-----------------------



1. CROSS JOIN
2. INNER JOIN
3. OUTER JOIN -> 1. LEFT OUTER JOIN

&#x09;	  2. RIGHT OUTER JOIN

4\. SELF JOIN

5\. NATURAL JOIN



1. CROSS JOIN /CARTESIAN JOIN:-

\--------------------------------

IT IS USED TO MERGE THE RECORDS OF ONE TABLE WITH THE RECORDS OF ANOTHER TABLE.





SYNTAX:-



SELECT COLUMN\_NAME/EXPRESSION

FROM TABLE\_NAME T1 CROSS JOIN TABLE\_NAME T2;





1. ##### WQTD CARTESIAN PRODUCT FROM EMPS AND LOCATIONS?

SELECT LID

##### INNER JOIN:-

\------------------



IT IS USED TO PRINT ONLY MATCHED FROM DIFFERENT TABLES.



✅ Short Answer

JOIN = INNER JOIN



##### SYNTAX:**-**

\------------



SELECT CLOUMN\_NAME /EXPRESSION

FROM TABLE\_NAME T1 INNER JOIN TABLE\_NAME T2

ON JOIN CONDTION;





###### ON :- IT IS KEYWORD USED TO JOIN CONSTION.



JOIN\_CONDTION:- IT IS A CONDION USED TO CONNECT MULTIPLE TABLES.



###### SYNTAX:-



T1.CLOUMN\_NAME=T2.COLUMN\_NAME

##### 2.WQTD ITEM NAME AND RESTAURANT NAME IF ITEM NAME IS FISH CURRY?

FROM MENU\_ITEMS I INNER JOIN RESTAURANTS R

ON I.RESTAURANT\_ID=R.RESTAURANT\_ID

##### 3\. WQTD CUSTOMER NAME AND THEIR ORDER DETAILS IF THEIR PAYMENT IS PENDING AND ORDER STATUS IS ACCEPTED?



**WRONG-**(

&#x09;SELECT C.NAME, O.\*,

&#x09;FROM CUSTOMERS C INNER JOIN ORDERS O

&#x09;ON C.ORDER\_ID=O.ORDER\_ID

&#x09;WHERE STATUS IN("PENDING","ACCEPTED");)





CORRECT:-



SELECT C.NAME, O.\*,P.STATUS

FROM CUSTOMERS C INNER JOIN ORDERS O INNER JOIN PAYMENTS P

ON C.ORDER\_ID=O.ORDER\_ID AND O.ORDER\_ID=P.ORDER\_ID

WHERE P.STATUS="PENDING" AND O.STATUS="ACCEPTED";





#### 4\. WQTD EMPS FNAME WHO DELIVERED MOST ORDERS?





SELECT E.FNAME

FROM EMPS E

INNER JOIN ORDERS O

ON E.EID = O.EID

WHERE O.STATUS = 'Delivered'

GROUP BY E.EID, E.FNAME

ORDER BY COUNT(\*) DESC

LIMIT 1;
